An award-winning, high-fidelity homepage for an intimate art gallery called "Heart's Creation" by artist Arunima Jain. The page should feel like stepping into a private art collection — warm, sophisticated, and emotionally resonant.

**DESIGN SYSTEM (REQUIRED):**
- Platform: Web, Desktop-first with mobile responsive
- Palette: Warm Cream (#FAF6F1) background, Deep Charcoal (#2A2A2A) text, Wine (#8B2E4A) single accent
- Typography: Cormorant Garamond for display/headlines (italic, light weight), DM Sans for body/UI (light, geometric)
- Styles: Minimal roundness (sharp edges for artwork frames, rounded-full for search), no heavy shadows, whisper borders (1px charcoal/5)
- Atmosphere: Intimate art gallery, warm linen tones, sophisticated restraint, slow graceful motion
- Motion: Framer Motion spring physics, staggered grid reveals, 500ms+ transitions
- Identity: Artist Arunima Jain's private studio — themes of silence, texture, and emotional depth

**PAGE STRUCTURE:**
1. **Fixed Navigation:** Floating cream/80 backdrop-blur navbar with rounded logo on the left, "Heart's Creation" in Cormorant Garamond, and navigation links (Home, Shop, About, Contact) on the right in uppercase DM Sans tracking-widest
2. **Hero Section (Full Viewport):** A cinematic, full-screen hero with a background video layer at 15% opacity with mix-blend-multiply showing an elephant walking. Over the video: a large, elegant SVG-style wordmark "Heart's Creation" in Cormorant Garamond italic, followed by the tagline "There is art in every heart" in italic serif, and a single CTA button "Explore the Collection" with a border-only style. A subtle animated scroll indicator line at the bottom.
3. **Featured Artworks Section:** A curated grid of 4-6 artwork images with hover zoom (scale-105, 1000ms transition). Each card shows the artwork image in a 4:5 aspect ratio, the title in serif below, and the medium in small uppercase tracking text. The section header reads "Selected Works" in uppercase tracking-[0.3em] charcoal/40 text.
4. **Artist Statement:** A centered, asymmetric section with a large italic Cormorant Garamond quote about art and the heart, followed by a small "— Arunima Jain" attribution.
5. **Footer:** Warm cream background with charcoal/5 top border. Three columns: Brand info with tagline, Navigation links, and Social/Connect links. Bottom bar with copyright and subtle "Site by Arunima" text.
