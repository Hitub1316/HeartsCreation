# Site Vision: Heart's Creation

## 1. Overview
**Heart's Creation** is an intimate online art gallery and studio by artist **Arunima Jain**. The website serves as a digital extension of her private collection — a place where art is not just displayed, but felt. The brand identity centers on warmth, emotional depth, and sophisticated restraint.

## 2. Brand Identity
- **Artist:** Arunima Jain
- **Tagline:** "There is art in every heart."
- **Themes:** Silence Blooming, Fragmented Thoughts, Moments Between Worlds
- **Aesthetic:** Rich maroons, deep textures, metallic whispers, linen warmth

## 3. Tech Stack
- **Framework:** Next.js 16 (App Router)
- **CMS:** Sanity.io
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS + Framer Motion
- **AI:** Gemini 2.0 Flash (Art Consultant)
- **Fonts:** Cormorant Garamond (serif) + DM Sans (sans)

- The wine accent (#8B2E4A) is the brand's signature color
- All transitions should feel slow, graceful, and intentional (500ms+)
